About

HEP Pencil - Higgens Eraser Pencil

The Higgens Eraser Pencil was created to be able to pivot your pencil, rather than turn it over to erase.
As a big fan of working Soduko puzzles on paper, it was frustrating to keep flipping the pencil over when filling numbers in and then erasing ones that weren't needed. It seemed obvious that a pencil with the lead and eraser at the same end would be easier to use. Since that product didn't exist, I decided to make one.

I hope you enjoy using yours!
